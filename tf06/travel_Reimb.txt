Travel Reimbursement



For International Participants
-----------------------------------

*Travel Reimbursement for winners (1st position) will be given on economy fare air ticket starting from a minimum of Rs 20,000 to full fare subject to availability of funds per team

*Travel Reimbursement will be given to a maximum of 2 persons per team.


For Participants from India
----------------------------------

*Travel Reimbursement for one way fare will be given for train (3rd AC / 2nd Sleeper) or bus fare (AC / Non-Ac).

*Travel Reimbursement will be given to a maximum of 3 persons per team.
