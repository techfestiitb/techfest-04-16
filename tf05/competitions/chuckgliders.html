Chuck Gliders

Make a glider out of Balsa wood, which when projected from a launching platform glides in air for the longest duration of time. The launching platform also has to be made by the participant.

Total cash prize of Rs 9,000 to be won!

Eligibility

Students currently enrolled for up to an undergraduate/postgraduate program at their institute are eligible for the competition.

Team Size

Number of members per team should not exceed two.

Judging Criteria

The glider with the maximum time of flight is declared as the winner. The time of flight is the time taken by the glider, from the instant it is launched, till it hits the ground.

General Rules

  • The glider should not use power from any source other than the launching platform.
  • The glider should not be connected to or controlled by any external element other than the atmospheric air.
  • Non traditional, unconventional innovative designs will be appreciated.
  • Every team has to register online at our website for the competition. A registration number will be allocated to the team on registration which shall be used for future reference.
  • On the request from the participants, supply of Balsa wood will be arranged, on payment of the required amount.
  • Judges decision shall be treated as final and binding on all.
  • Techfest shall not be responsible for any damages to the material (balsa, foam etc) sent to the participants.
  • For information regarding the availability of balsa and other material: click here
  • The last date for registration is 5th Jan 2005.
