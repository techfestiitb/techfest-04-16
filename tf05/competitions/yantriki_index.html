YANTRIKI - The Robotics Extravaganza

Started in 1995 as an intra IIT competition, Yantriki has grown to become the "Largest robotics event in India". Over the years it has seen the most interesting of problem statements that offer young robotics enthusiasts and connoisseurs the platform to gain invaluable experience both with the technology and the practical elements that go into the making/automation of a robot and has featured intriguing workshops and lectures providing insight into the field of robotics. And what's more? It's a whole lot of fun!

The problem statements push you forward to break the shackles and think beyond the limits where human constraints are futile. It challenges both man and machine to excel not only in technology but also in strategies and innovation. They help discover that nascent talent in you. They are designed to teach teamwork, problem solving, project management, and taking pride in task completion.

Yantriki in the year 2005 promises to come up with competitions full of innovation. So what are you waiting for, GET, SET, and FREE your imagination and let it roam wild. The World is out there and is waiting for you... Show the world you can do everything and can convert every 'nothing' into 'everything'.

SURVIVOR - The International Challenge 

Make a wireless remote controlled machine, which travels up a slope to reach a platform 1 m high; then jumps off the platform to reach the ground; completes a task involving some blocks; and then follows a line in an autonomous fashion. The machine fetching itself more number of points at the end of the match is declared as the winner.

Micromouse  

Build a small self-contained robot (micromouse) to negotiate a maze in the shortest possible time.

Rescue 

You are challenged to take the princess to a castle atop a hill. But there is a moat in your way which you have to fill up and complete the path.

Junior(for for students upto 10+2) 

Design a vehicle (mechanism) which can move a 500 ml Sprite (beverage) bottle into a zone situated at a distance, in the same plane.

Power Boats 

Make a wireless remote controlled boat, which travels around a racing circuit on water in the shortest possible time. The basic requirements for the boats are speed and agility.
