Decathalon

What is decathlon ?

It is a collection of events, covering various aspects of science and technology. There will be different tech-tasks, which the teams have to accomplish and earn points. The team with the maximum number of points at the end of the competition wins. The only prerequisite - great teamwork.

Total cash prize of Rs 12,000 to be won!

What are these tech-tasks ?

These are different tasks related to various fields like electronics, electrical engg, computer science, mechanical engg, communication and presentation skills etc.

Who can participate ?

All students with a valid identity card of their respective institutes.

How big my team may be ?

The number of team members must not be less than 5 or more than 6.

How do I be a part of it ?

step 1 : make a good team
step 2 : register - click here
step 3 : clear the qualifying round
step 4 : come to Techfest 2005

How will the qualifying round be conducted ?

  • The last date for registration is 25th Dec 2005.
  • After the registrations close, you will be sent a set of questions by e-mail. You have to answer those questions and submit them within the specified date.
  • On the basis of their scores in this test, teams will be selected to participate in Decathlon at Techfest 2005. Here, please note that NO MORE THAN TWO TEAMS from the same college/institute will be shortlisted.
  • The shortlisted teams will be sent a confirmation by e-mail.
