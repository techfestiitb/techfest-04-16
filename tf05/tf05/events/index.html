Events

True to the spirit of a festival, Techfest encompasses a plethora of Events that will put you in awe of Technology and Science. In compliance with our philosophy of 'Technology for All', we believe in both testing of skills and imparting of knowledge on a wide range of fields be it Yantriki, Last Straw and Chemsplash for matching the wits, or a series of Lectures by eminent personalities giving a piece of their mind. And for the glamour hungry techies we give you Technoholix - Techfest at Dark.

In the past Techfest has had the distinction of providing students with an opportunity of face-to-face interaction with the gurus of modern Industry and Academia. List of our eminent guests includes the likes of Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam, Dr. R. Chidambaram, Mr. Rajat Gupta (McKinsey and Co.), Dr. Raghuram Rajan (IMF), Mr. Narayanamurthy (Infosys), Mr. Richard Stallman, Prof. Kevin Warwick, Prof. Mohanbir Sawhney (Kellogs, GSM), media guru Alyque Padamsee, Prof. Yash Pal and Dr. Jayant Narlikar among others.

"Learning while working" has been the main ideology behind Techfest. And hence for the die-hard tech geeks Techfest features Yantriki - the ultimate in Robotics Competition, Last Straw - a civil engineering competition, and Chemsplash - a unique event for Chemical Engineers. All these events have come a long way since their inception and their reputation put together with astounding sums of prizes make them the highlight of Techfest.

Exit light. Enter Technoholix - Techfest at Dark . After a draining day of competitions, Technoholix is nothing but Entertainment Redefined. Fasten your seat belts cause we'll take you to a whole new world of cutting-edge technology paired with glamour in it's crudest form. Over the years, a massive crowd of more than 5000 people have been left spellbound by an amazing display of 3-D laser and a spectacular game of soccer played by Sony Aibo robots, who had the crowd literally dancing with them for every goal scored.

Events at Techfest 2005 promise you an unforgettable tryst with Science and Technology. Be there or regret!
