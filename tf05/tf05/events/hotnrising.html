Hot & Rising

Ever thought how a hot air balloon works?? Now learn yourself by making a hot air balloon of your own.

Problem Statement

Make a hot air balloon powered by commonly available candles. The balloon has to lift the maximum amount of load to a height of atleast 10 meters. The balloon can be made of materials like paper, plastics, etc.

Eligibility

Students currently enrolled for up to an undergraduate/postgraduate program at their institute are eligible for the competition.

Team Size

Number of members per team should not exceed two.

General Rules

  • The balloon should fit a cube of size 1mx1mx1m at all times during the competition. Any team exceeding the size criteria will be penalised.
  • Only commonly available candles should be used as a source of power for the balloons. Use of highly flammable fuels will attract strict penalty. Proper safety precautions should be taken while preparing for this event.
  • There should be a provision of attaching a tether/thread to the balloon. The tether will restrict the balloon from flying off. The teams whose balloons are lost(fly away, and cant be recovered.), will be disqualified.
  • Every team has to register online at our website for the competition. A registration number will be allocated to the team on registration which shall be used for future reference.
  • The balloon should rise to the specified height in less than 10 minutes. Rise time of more than 10 minutes will not be valid.
  • Judges decision shall be treated as final and binding on all.
  • The last date for registration is 5th Jan 2005.
