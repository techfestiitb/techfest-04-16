Scitech

"How many 1cc cubes can you fit into a single 2cc cube?"

Simple enough isn't it? Try it with a spotlight on your face and 6000 right faces staring at you. The Scitech Olympiad will start your grey cells ticking and send them into overdrive. No tedious facts. No boring trivia. All that is needed is a passion for science and technology, a sharp mind and loads of courage.

The Scitech Olympiad is the largest quiz of its genre in India. With a participant base that spans the top techincal institutes in the country, this years winners will walk away with the brand new Scitech Olympiad Trophy, an ample pay-cheque and the satisfaction of having beaten the best.

The 6 finalists are chosen from over 200 participants through a written elimination. The Scitech finals are among the most awaited events at Techfest each year. The finals are viewed by over 6000 students filled into IIT Bombay's Open Air Theatre. Scitech veterans will tell you that nothing can be as disconcerting as the spotlight on your face and nothing more rewarding than the cheering when you do get an answer right.

This year's finals will be hosted by Barry O'Brien, among India's most acclaimed quiz masters and a veteran of over 1500 quizzes.
