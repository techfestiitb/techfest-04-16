Assistance and Hospitality

Over the years, Techfest has grown in stature, both in terms of its content, as well as its logistics. The dream, which had humble beginnings, has come to become a technical bonanza, drawing crowds numbering in tens of thousands. When one talks of such magnitudes, hospitality of the guests becomes a primary concern! Techfest tries its level best to satisfy one and all.

Right from the moment you land here at Mumbai, we will take care of all your needs during your stay at Techfest 2005. Transport facility will be made available for our visitors from various railway stations in Mumbai to IITB campus. Our people at assistance desks, situated at every corner of IITB campus, will be very happy to help you out.

Here at Techfest, we try to provide accommodation to as many people as possible. Accommodation will be provided only to students/faculty of institutes outside Mumbai. Accommodation is provided to the Participants prior to the Visitors.

Participants are those who have a confirmation to participate in any of the competitions or workshops. All the other students who are not participants are Visitors. Note that for the competitions/workshops where there is a pre-elimination, mere registration for that event does not make you a participant at Techfest 2005.

How to avail accommodation?

For Participants

1. All Participants need to register for their respective competition(s) or workshop at www.techfest.org/register before the last date of registration.

For competitions without pre-elimination
i.  Participants will receive an Application of Accommodation e-mail on registering for the competition.
ii.  You are required to take the print-out of the Application of Accommodation e-mail and obtain the signature (with stamp) of the Dean/Head of your Institute. One copy of the same should be sent to us (by post/fax) at the address mentioned below before 8th January, 2005. In case you do not want to avail accommodation, you need not send the above mentioned letter.
iii   The receipt of the above letter will be sent to you by e-mail at the earliest.
iv.  The final Confirmation of Accommodation mail shall be sent to you by 10th January, 2005.

For competitions with pre-elimination

     i.  The final short listed Participants will receive a Confirmation of Accommodation mail after the pre-elimination results are declared.
     ii.   There is no need to send any fax/letter for availing accommodation.

2. The Confirmation of Accommodation mail shall contain necessary details regarding your accommodation at Techfest 2005.
3. Accommodation will be provided only when the printout of the final Confirmation of Accommodation mail is produced at the time of availing accommodation.
4. There should be one and only one form per team. For teams taking part in more than one competition, only one form is sufficient.

For Visitors


1. To apply as Visitors, you need to fill the Visitors Accommodation Form. The completed forms must be submitted at the earliest.
2. Accommodation to Visitors will be provided on a second preference after participants as per availability.
3. All visitors whose accommodation is confirmed will receive a Confirmation of Accommodation mail latest by 15th January, 2005.
4. Accommodation will be provided only when the printout of the final Confirmation of Accommodation mail is produced at the time of availing accommodation.
5. No on-the-spot requests shall be entertained.
6. There should be only one form per institute.

Faculty members attending the festival should kindly inform us well in advance. A Confirmation of Accommodation e-mail will be sent by us.

Note :

1. You must bring your college identity cards in order to avail accommodation. Please note that no other document (a letter or otherwise) will be accepted.
2. Accommodation will be provided from 6:00 AM on 27th January, 2005 onwards. You are strictly required to vacate the rooms by 10:00 AM on 31st January, 2005.
3. Accommodation will be provided on a sharing basis.
4. A refundable security deposit and nominal fee shall be charged per person for the accommodation provided. Details regarding the exact amount will be made available on the website soon.

Feel free to mail us at assistance@techfest.org for clarifications regarding accommodation.
