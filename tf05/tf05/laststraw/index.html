Constructa

Design a bridge using ice-cream sticks as the building material and satisfying constraints mentioned below.

^ TOP

Eligibility

 All students with a valid identity card of their respective institutes are eligible to participate in constructa at Techfest 2005.

^ TOP

Team Size

 A team can consist of a maximum of three students.

^ TOP

Design Constraints and General Rules

The participants must follow the following guidelines for maximum and minimum bridge dimensions and other specifications to avoid disqualification:

The design consists of two parts:

1) The minimum length of the bridge should be 60 cm.
2) The minimum operating width of the deck should lie between 15 cm and 20 cm .
3) The minimum height of the bridge deck from the ground should be 35 cm.

Definition of Failure: If structure fails to carry the load carrying trolley over its deck.

NO REPAIRS MAY TAKE PLACE ONCE THIS TEST IS STARTED. ANY TEAM CAUGHT DOING SO WILL BE DISQUALIFIED.

4) A smooth continuous bridge deck for vehicular traffic must be provided along the entire span of the bridge.
5) A moving load will be applied on the deck of the bridge.
6) The Bridge is to be built in advance.
7) The materials specified are standard ice-cream sticks, standard adhesive, threads and cello/packing tape. Teams using unapproved materials will be disqualified.
8)Threads can be used only at the joints and not as a structural member .
9) The standard wooden ice-cream sticks to be used should have following dimensions:
Length : 10cm - 12cm
Breadth : 1cm - 1.5cm
Height : 1mm - 3mm

Bridge Evaluation Criteria

The team with maximum resistance factor is considered winner. Resistance Factor is calculated as,

Resistance Factor = Ultimate Load resisted / (Dead weight of bridge)2
