Communique

With its panoramic spread of lectures and discussions, debates and competitions, Communique tends to be more potent than a heavy dose of caffeine. From current rages to age old topics, the gurus of modern science & technology and industry give you a piece of their mind, while you get a chance to express your own ideas as well.

Lecture Series, boasts of an impressive list of speakers over the years including names like Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam, Hon. President of India, Dr. Jayant Narlikar, Mr. Narayana Murthy (Chairman, Infosys),Mr. Rajat Gupta, Prof. Yash Pal, Prof. Kevin Warwick, Prof. Alex Pentland and media guru Alyque Padamsee, besides, we also had video conferences with Dr. Richard M. Stallman (Founder of GNU Project and Free Software Foundation), Mr. Sam Pitroda (Father of Telecommunications in India), eminent astrophysicist Sir Roger Penrose and Dr. Paul Schuch (Executive Director, SETI), Dr. Raghuram Rajan, Chief Economist of the International Monetary Fund (IMF).

Incase you are one to express your views then we got panel discussions, covering the latest topics in science and technology. Don't go anywhere, because that's not all there is to Lecture Series. This year we got something special waiting for all of you, namely a podium and a lot of attentive ears!
