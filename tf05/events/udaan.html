Udaan

There probably have been many times when you've looked up at the skies and watched the birds glide gracefully and without any trace of effort. Times when you've been awed by the scale and the marvel that is flight. Hours you've spent gazing at posters of planes looking at the intricate design.

If planes are any part of your life, if the forefront of Technology is where you want to be (believe it or not, technology that is introduced in planes takes more than a decade to percolate into other fields, be it automobiles, electronics or even some aspects of computer software), Techfest 2005 is proud to invite you to be a part of Udaan, the Aeronautics extravaganza.

Udaan has events that are sure to keep anyone even with a slight interest in the fascinating field of Aeronautics glued to their seats with their thinking caps on and in full swing.....
