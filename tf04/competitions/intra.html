INTRA IIT EVENTS

Competitions at Techfest are aimed to pit students against the best opposition in the country. The challenges in the competition try not only stimulate and bring out the creative genius in the participants but also make it a fun and learning experience for the participants.

In this endeavour of exposing the student to more and more interesting and innovative stuff, the competitions within IIT Bombay are not restricted to just the 3 days of Techfest but run through out the year as intra-IIT competitions. While a few of them provide a flavour to what will be unvieled in Techfest, most of them are run-ups to the major events like Yantriki, Chemsplash, Last Straw etc. The intra-IIT events at Techfest 2004 include:

la porsChe - Chemsplash Intra:

Chemsplash (Techfest 2004) proudly present its second Intra IIT Competition on 17th January, 2004.
Line Drafter - Yantriki Intra:
Line Drafter, the first Yantriki intra IIT event of this year, comprised of designing a vehicle/mechanism which can draw ten parallel straight lines (2 cm. apart and a lenngth of approximately 6 cm.) with the help of pen or pencil. The winner was decided on the parallelism and consistency of lines.
Quake - Laststraw Intra:

The Last Straw Intra IIT Competition "Quake" consisted of designing a structure using straws that carries maximum load under a specific earthquake base excitation. The event witnessed an unprecedented turn out with more that sixty teams submitting their structures for testing. The overall involvement and enthusiasm level was also very high among the participants.
