ATLANTI SAMPLE SOLUTION

Consider a sample problem, with the following distribution of area and population:

  1. AREA DISTRIBUTION
    • Total Area: 25 km. square
    • Area Occupied by Physical features: 35%
    • Area available for building city: 65%


  2. POPULATION DISTRIBUTION
    • Total Population: 2,500
    • Fraction going to Industrial Area: 0.33
    • Fraction going to Commercial Area: 0.25
    • Fraction going to Agricultural Area: 0.02

  3. DENSITY DISTRIBUTION
    • Density of Residential Area: 625 people/km. square
    • Density of Industrial Area: 300 people/km. square
    • Density of Commercial Area: 250 people/km. square
    • Density of Agricultural Area: 20 people/km. square

  4. HENCE AREA DISTRIBUTION FOR GIVEN CITY
    • Residential Area: 4 km. square
    • Industrial Area: 2.75 km. square
    • Commercial Area: 2.5 km. square
    • Agricultural Area: 2.5 km. square

  5. With the above distribution in mind, we constructed a city in which each area was divided into different zones as shown:
    • Residential area into 3 zones:
      • R1 of area 2 square km. and population 1250.
      • R2 of area 1 square km. and population 625.
      • R3 of area 1 square km. and population 625.
    • Agricultural area into 2 zones:
      • A1 of area 1.25 square km. and population 375.
      • A2 of area 1.5 square km. and population 450.
    • Commercial area into 2 zones:
      • C1 of area 1.5 square km. and population 375.
      • C2 of area 1 square km. and population 250.
    • Industrial area into 2 zones:
      • I1 of area 1.5 square km. and population 30.
      • I2 of area 1 square km. and population 20.
