TECHFEST 2004

Techfest is the annual science and technology festival of IIT Bombay. From a fledgling in 1998, today it has grown to be the largest fest of its kind in entire Asia. Techfest enjoys a participation of about 15,000 students from over 750 colleges across India and over 1500 faculty and corporate executives. In its seventh avatar, Techfest 2004 is going international and is scheduled from 24th-26th January, 2004.

Techfest portrays the vivid spectrum of science and technology and how it is affecting our every-day lives. Whether it is the latest and the best in R&D, lectures by some of the most eminent personalities from myriad fields like science, technology and management, innovative and exciting competitions or workshops on riveting topics, they are all to be found in the Techfest of IIT Bombay.

It is of utmost importance to realize that boundaries are mere perceptions. The shattering of constraints and barriers imposed on oneself is the first step to realizing the boundless infinity of the fascinating world of science and technology. Creative thought and vision sow the seed of progress - but it is opportunity and constant endeavour which nourishes development.

Techfest seeks to initiate and further this spirit in the vibrant and dynamic student community. Techfest 2004 endeavors to bear aloft the torch of the future which has been lit and passed down through six years of Techfest. We invite you to share our dream and realize its fruition with us.
