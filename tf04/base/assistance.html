ASSISTANCE
People seldom refuse help if one offers it in the right way.

Assistance Desk

Right from the point you enter the lush green IIT campus, we shall be there to guide you through the maze of competitions, workshops, lectures happening at Techfest to make sure you are always where you want to be! There shall be Assistance Desks at all critical points in the campus during the day to attend to your queries regarding schedules, campus map and the various events spread across the campus. In a nutshell, people here will know anything and everything you don't know but need to know.


Travel Reimbursements

Techfest shall not provide travel reimbursement to any team for the journey from their home town to Bombay. However, transportation for the teams from the major stations to the campus will be available free of cost.


Transportation

We shall have buses plying at regular intervals from the major stations in Bombay to IIT Powai. The schedule of the buses shall be put up on the website as the fest approaches. You can mail us your time of arrival in Bombay to help us plan better.


How to get to IIT Powai

To get to IIT Powai, just remember that Kanjurmarg is the nearest local station and it lies on the Central Railway line.
The address is simply IIT Powai, Bombay . 400076.
Following is a rough outline of how to get to IIT Powai from the major train stations.

Alight (preferably in order) at :

  • Dadar - Take a slow train on the central line to Kanjurmarg station and then take an auto rickshaw to IIT (approx fare Rs.14/-).


  • Bandra - Take a fast local train to Dadar station from Bandra local station and follow the instructions in (a).


  • Bombay Central - Take a local train to Andheri followed by BEST bus (bus nos. 396, 392, 336) to IIT main gate from Andheri East OR take a local train to Dadar and follow the instructions in (a).


  • Lokmanya Tilak Terminus (Kurla) - Take an auto rickshaw to IIT Main Gate (approx fare 75-90) OR catch the slow local to Kanjurmarg from Kurla local station followed by an auto rickshaw to IIT (approx fare Rs. 14/-).


Accommodation

Accommodation will be provided from 6:00 a.m., 23rd January to 12 noon, 27th January, 2004. We suggest that you are here by 9 a.m. on 24th January to enjoy the fest right from the start.

Accommodation Desk is the place to reach once you enter the IIT campus. Don't worry, we shall have people and banners guiding you all the way. This desk is open 24 hours a day for all your emergency needs. We suggest you go through the Accommodation Policy as under for the complete details.

Accommodation Policy

Techfest endeavors to provide accommodation to as many people as possible. Accommodation will be provided only to students/faculty of institutes outside Mumbai.

Accommodation is provided on a priority basis first to Participants and then to Visitors. Participants are those who have a confirmation to participate in any of the competitions or workshops. Visitors are those who do not have a confirmation for any of the competition or workshop. Accommodation is provided on a first come first serve basis to Visitors as per constraints.

 Participants:

  • All Participants have to register for their respective competitions at www.techfest.org. Last date for registration was 10th January. You may still register for some of the competitions. But this registration would not guarantee accommodation. You shall receive a mail after 18th January giving you further details.
  • All confirmed Participants would be intimated of their confirmation of accommodation by a Confirmation of Accommodation mail ina couple of days.
  • For competitions/workshops where there is a pre-elimination through abstracts/video, the final confirmed Participants will automatically receive the above Confirmation of Accommodation mail after the elimination results are declared. There is no need to send the letter signed by the Head of your institute.
  • Please note that only those who have a printout of this Confirmation of Accommodation mail will be provided accommodation.
 Visitors:
  • Last date for receiving visitor form was 10th January. From now on, no more forms would be entertained.
  • Accommodation to visitors will be provided on a second preference after participants as per availability.
  • All visitors whose accommodation is confirmed will receive a Confirmation of Accommodation mail. Please note that only those who have a printout of this mail will be provided accommodation.
  • There should be only one form per institute for visitors.

Faculty members attending the festival should kindly inform us in advance. Accommodation will be provided to faculty members only after confirmation from our side.


Points to remember

  • You must have a printout of the Confirmation of Accommodation mail sent by us to avail accommodation.

  • You must also bring your college identity cards in order to avail accommodation. Please note that no other document (a letter or otherwise) will be accepted.

  • Accommodation will be provided from 6:00 a.m. on 23th January'04 to 12:00 noon of 27th January'04.

  • Accommodation will be provided on a sharing basis.

  • A refundable security deposit and nominal fees may be charged per person for the accommodation provided. Details regarding this exact amount will be available on the website soon.
