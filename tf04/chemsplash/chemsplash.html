CHEMSPLASH

The Chemical Engineering Extravaganza

Chemsplash is THE annual event for chem enthusiasts. From what started of as a mere idea in the year 2000 has blossomed into an event which is now the biggest event of its kind in India.It now boasts of participation of 200 teams from all over the country, battling over competitions like Chem-e-car, Turbulence (The quiz contest) and TechnumOpus (The poster presentation contest) for prizes worth 50 grand. This year, we have introduced a new event called High Spirits which is bound to stimulate your grey cells!

A brief description of the events we have in Chemplash 2004 :


la porsChe:

The objective of this highly successful competition is to design and construct a car that is powered with a chemical energy source that will carry a load to a given distance and stop by exhausting the power source or cutting of the power without the application of brakes or electronic mechanism (so here you need to trust those whimsical chemicals!!!) The car to stand such a scare should be robust and yet pleasing to the eyes! To know how we decide on such an ultimate car, check out the rules.
Cash Prizes of Rs. 24,000more . . .
High Spirits:

Ever wondered how that lift manages to take you to the 80th floor of the Empire State Building? That one's easy...just electricity. At TechFest, however, everything works differently, doesn't it? You will have to design a lift that will raise a load through a specified height. The catch is, it has to be powered purely by chemicals.
Cash Prizes of Rs. 19,000more . . .

TechnumOpus:

This is the poster presentation contest at Chemsplash. Here you get an opportunity to present a paper on any topic related to the world of chemistry / chemical engineering. Entries will be short listed based upon an abstract which all the teams have to send a month before Techfest commences.
Cash Prizes of Rs. 16,000more . . .

Dexter's Den:

Einstein was once asked 'Assume that the world is going to end an hour, how would you react' The great soul was quick to reply, 'I'd mathematically model the problem in the first fifty-nine minutes so as to get a one minute solution to the problem'. Though we do not know the degree of truth in this anecdote we can surely offer you a chance to play Einstein, albeit a chemical engineering one. Chemsplash is proud to unravel for the first time Dexter's Den.
Cash Prizes of Rs. 10,000more . . .
Turbulence:

This is the quiz competition at ChemSplash. It consists of a written eliminations round, followed by the finals, which will consist of several interesting rounds like visual round, buzzer round, the normal questions round and rapid fire at the end.
Cash Prizes of Rs. 8,000more . . .

What is the driving force behind Chemsplash?

Chemical Engineering is a creation of the 20th Century. We have had a century in which Chemical engineering has blossomed, developed, transformed itself, and perhaps matured. We are now in a new century, Chemical Engineering's second century, and already challenges are driving vast changes in our industries, their methods of operation, their requirements of Chemical Engineers-indeed,society's requirements of Chemical Engineers-and the educational approaches and tools we can and need to use. The chemical process Industry has been the engine behind the growth of wealth of nations. The global chemical industry is transforming itself by placing at its centre the concerns for human safety and welfare, environmental protection, energy conservation and higher efficiency. And in this endeavor of reinventing itself the chemical industry is rapidly imbibing the benefits of the ongoing Information Technology revolution. In short, our profession is in a state of heady flux! And thus, our times can at once appear confusing as well as stupendously potent! So, what are the dominant evolutionary strands today in the chemical engineering profession? We need to reflect and sift the true from the apparent. This real world practice of our profession is made in the events of Chemsplash, which itself is a true handmaiden of progress.
