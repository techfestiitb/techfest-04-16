DEXTER's DEN

Einstein was once asked 'Assume that the world is going to end in an hour, how would you react' The great soul was quick to reply, 'I'd mathematically model the problem in the first fifty-nine minutes so as to get a one minute solution to the problem'. Though we do not know the degree of truth in this anecdote we can surely offer you a chance to play Einstein, albeit a chemical engineering one. Chemsplash is proud to unravel for the first time Dexter's Den. The event will comprise of two stages:

Total Cash Prize of Rs. 10,000 to be won.

Stage 1: Mathmodel

  1. The participants will be provided with three problem statements at the start of the day of the event. Participants will be required to mathematically/qualitatively model a solution to one of the problems. The solutions will be expected by the end of the day.
  2. This will only be an elimination round and no points from this stage would carry over to the next.
  3. Based on the mathematical model that the teams have proposed five teams will be selected for the next round. The judge's decision in this regard will be final and binding.
  4. In case the team has provided more than one solution, the best solution will be considered for stage 2.

Stage 2: Implementing the model

  1. The teams will have to implement their model in this stage of the competition. The necessary apparatus, materials and chemicals for this purpose will be provided.
  2. The exact details of these will have been made clear in the first stage and the teams would be expected to base their solution utilizing these and no more materials. Violation of this rule in would lead to disqualification of the team. The best solution for each problem will be declared the winner.

General Rules:

  1. Teams for this event could consist of one or two members.

  2. For this event, no pre-registrations are required. Participants will have to register on the spot.

  3. Judges decision shall be treated as final and binding on all.
