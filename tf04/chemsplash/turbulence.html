Why is the fundamental question?....Forty two is the answer. Doesn't matter if you did'nt get this piece of trivia as long as you know your chemical engineering.

Chemsplash presents 'Turbulence-The Quiz Contest'. You have been slogging it out all this while in chemical engineering and maybe formed the opinion that the field is lacklustre. Well,we are out to prove just the opposite. Now is the opportunity to beat that nerdy topper from the class at a game of skill and not rote. We are here for fun and we promise you all loads of it. So just pick up a pen and a couple of friends and just check in.

Total Cash Prize of Rs. 8,000 to be won.

General Rules:

  1. The quiz competition will be held in two rounds.

  2. The first round will be for screening purpose and will be a written one. Questions of objective type, a few involving audio visuals etc should be expected.

  3. Top six teams will go through the second and final round.

  4. A team of two is allowed.

  5. There would be on the spot registration for the quiz. No pre-registration are required for this event.

Great prizes in cash and kind to be won.

Also check out our online quizzes to get a feel of the real experience.
